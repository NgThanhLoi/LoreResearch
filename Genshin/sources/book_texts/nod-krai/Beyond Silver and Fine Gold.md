'''Beyond Silver and Fine Gold''' is a World Quest Series about Nicole.

Requirements: 28;Witch's Lodge (Quest).

==Quest Details==
* Once the World Quest is available, the player can follow the questline to explore the rooms within the Witch's Lodge. Each room must be fully explored to unlock the next.
* After completing the exploration of the current room, the subsequent room will unlock at 04:00 on the following Monday.
* It will take 4 weeks to complete the entire "Beyond Silver and Fine Gold" quest.
