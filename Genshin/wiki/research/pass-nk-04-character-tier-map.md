# Nod-Krai Character Tier Map (Pass NK-04)

This tier map categorizes characters related to Nod-Krai into distinct operational tiers. This classification dictates their priority, allowed actions, and integration paths within the global Full Character Foundation Plan.

---

## 1. Tier Definitions & Policy

- **Tier A — Future Foundation Candidates after Source Coverage Check**
  - *Criteria*: Local Nod-Krai characters supported by quest summary `AQ-NK-0001` or local books, ready for eventual regional writing after validation.
  - *Policy*: Keep at Level 0/1 inventory status. Validate source presence (voice lines, profile stories) in future global passes before upgrading.
  - *Forbidden Action*: Do NOT create character pages or draft personality/relationship details.

- **Tier B — Inventory / Source-Light Only**
  - *Criteria*: Historical, folkloric, or book-only characters with no active modern quest representation.
  - *Policy*: Reference only in regional history, mythology, or faction summaries. Maintain at Level 0/1.
  - *Forbidden Action*: Do NOT create character page files or individual persona profiles.

- **Tier C — Existing Global / High-Risk Characters; Do Not Duplicate**
  - *Criteria*: Playable or major global lore characters whose page ownership belongs strictly to the global Full Character Foundation Plan.
  - *Policy*: Map regional involvement and gaps. All modifications must be merged directly into the global plan's character pages.
  - *Forbidden Action*: Do NOT create duplicate local character pages or edit existing files in `wiki/entities/characters/`.

- **Tier D — Blocked / Source-Missing**
  - *Criteria*: Characters associated with Snezhnaya in theories or later acts (Acts II-VII) but having zero primary source support in the current workspace.
  - *Policy*: Strict quarantine. Keep blocked at Level 0/1 until physical sources are ingested.
  - *Forbidden Action*: Do NOT integrate any later-act claims (e.g. Dottore False Moon, Skirk Descender) into the wiki.

- **Tier E — Mention-Only / Background**
  - *Criteria*: Minor NPCs mentioned in quest summaries with no lore weight or active role.
  - *Policy*: Keep at Level 0. Reference only in subregion/quest event lists.
  - *Forbidden Action*: Do NOT write character pages or assign personality/relationship details.
