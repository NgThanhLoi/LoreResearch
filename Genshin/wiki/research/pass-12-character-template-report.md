## Template Design Basis

The template uses `wiki/entities/characters/Kaeya.md` as the positive structural model while avoiding Kaeya-specific claims. It preserves the useful section flow from Kaeya: audit status, source coverage, canon identity, personality tiers, OOC risk, voice, relationships, genre notes, safe/unsafe use, sources, and maintenance notes.

The design also incorporates guarded variants for source-light and blocked characters. This aligns with Pass 11's coverage plan, which identifies Dottore and Sandrone as blocked, Columbina as source-light, and Kaeya as the only Level 4 maintained character after the Pass 10 provenance and source-status work.

The template separates Level 2 foundation work from Level 3 provenance enhancement and Level 4 claim mapping. Future passes can therefore build useful character pages without pretending every page has Kaeya-level source coverage.
