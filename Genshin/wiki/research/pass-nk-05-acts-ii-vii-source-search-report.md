# Pass NK-05 — Nod-Krai / Snezhnaya Acts II–VII Raw Source Search Report

## Purpose
This report documents the search, verification, and provenance feasibility assessment for Nod-Krai / Snezhnaya Archon Quest Acts II–VII under the "Song of the Welkin Moon" chapter. The objective is to determine if raw or near-raw source records exist to unlock these acts from `source_missing` / `blocked_unavailable` status, or if they must remain blocked.

## Search Method
1. **Local Audit:** Inspected the local workspace directories, specifically `sources/voice_lines/archon_quests/`, `sources/book_texts/nod-krai/`, and `topics/nod-krai/` to identify existing files, structural shells, or templates.
2. **External Queries:** Performed targeted web searches on official HoYoverse/Genshin channels, the Genshin Impact Fandom Wiki, and the Genshin Impact Fanon Wiki using exact search terms such as `"Song of the Welkin Moon"`, `"Elegy of Dust and Lamplight"`, `"A Nation That Doesn't Exist"`, and specific act titles.
3. **HTML Extraction & Analysis:** Successfully fetched and parsed the live Fandom wiki page for "Song of the Welkin Moon" to audit its structure, quest listings, and source metadata.

## External Sources Searched
*   **Genshin Impact Official HoYoverse Channels:** No references to "Song of the Welkin Moon" as an Archon Quest chapter were found. Official quest list ends at Natlan / Snezhnaya prelude.
*   **Genshin Impact Fandom Wiki:** Contains pages for "Song of the Welkin Moon" and its associated acts, but explicitly states that **"Song of the Welkin Moon" is not an official chapter in the game's actual Archon Quest storyline**. It is identified as a fan-created or hypothetical expansion.
*   **Genshin Impact Fanon Wiki:** Confirmed as the host environment for the "Song of the Welkin Moon" creative project and its sub-acts.

## Acts Not Found
No official, raw in-game transcripts or developer quest logs exist for **any** of these acts.

## Transcript Availability by Act
*   **Prelude to Act X:** **None**. There are no dialogue transcripts, voice-line logs, or official text records. Only fan-written plot summaries and walkthrough objective lists exist on the wiki.

## Key Blockers
1.  **Fanon Nature:** The entire questline is a fan-created, community-driven storyline. It does not exist in the official Genshin Impact game files or official media.
2.  **No Raw Dialogue:** No text transcripts of character dialogue exist on the wiki pages.
3.  **Missing Local Files:** The local directories for these quests contain no text files.

## Summary Conclusion
Acts II–VII (and the additional acts VIII–X) are **completely blocked/unavailable** for the canon layer of the wiki. Unlocking them from `source_missing` status is impossible because they do not represent official game content and lack any primary raw transcripts. 

The external source candidates identified can only be classified as **Tier D (Summary Only / Do Not Canonize)**. They must remain quarantined from the main wiki pages to prevent fanon/theory contamination of official Genshin Impact lore.
