## Analysis Results

### 1. Claims with Evidence Found (`evidence_found_guarded` / `supports_candidate`)
* **acts_ii_vii**: Pinned revisions exist and physically contain all dialogues and stages.
* **columbina_kuutar**: Columbina / Damselette is linked to Kuutar Moon Maiden terminology in checked pinned revisions, but Alice/Arlecchino state neither 'Columbina' nor 'Kuutar' is her true name.
* **dottore_false_moon**: Dottore uses Moon Marrow to assume the form "Heretic of the False Moon" and usurp divine power.
* **false_sky_mechanics**: The false sky acts as a barrier that is physically shattered when the true moon is pulled into Teyvat.
* **true_welkin_moon**: The three moon sisters (Aria, Sonnet, Canon) are introduced as cosmic entities imprisoned by Nibelung.
* **moon_authority**: Primordial moon authority represents cosmic divine power sought by Dottore.
* **traveler_moon_marrow**: The Traveler acts as the custodian of the Moon Marrow (remains of Aria) and can channel its power.
* **voynich_guild**: Confirms the physical existence of the Voynich Guild as an active scholarly/technical faction in Snezhnaya.
* **nefer_boss_lady**: Confirms Nefer is the leader ("Boss Lady") of the Curatorium of Secrets.
* **nod_krai_later_locations**: Details geographic entities like Lempo Isle, Nasha Town, Hiisi Island, Lake Amsvartnir, and Silvermoon Hall.
* **harbinger_actions**: Details the Fatui command chain and internal conflicts between Dottore, Columbina, and Sandrone in Nod-Krai.

### 2. Claims with Partial / Ambiguous Evidence
* **dottore_irminsul_hack**: Evidence indicates Dottore targets and intercepts a local "Wanderer's Irminsul core hidden inside Pulonia," rather than hacking Sumeru's main Irminsul tree.
* **sandrone_khaenriah_link**: Sandrone's bureau is situated in a Khaenri'ahn ruin and her puppet Pulonia uses Khaenri'ahn design schools, but no direct lineage proof is available.
* **abyss_sibling_actions**: Sibling appearances in Act II are revealed to be phantoms generated by Rerir. Actual Sibling actions remain guarded.

### 3. Claims Not Found (`source_missing_after_deep_read`)
* **sandrone_rotwang**: Rotwang name absent from checked NK-07 pinned revisions.
* **skirk_descender**: Skirk name absent from checked NK-07 pinned revisions.

### 4. Claims Kept Blocked
* Any claims regarding **Rotwang** or **Skirk** remain strictly blocked.
* All other claims remain guarded at `evidence_found_guarded` and must not be treated as narrator-safe canon.
