# Pass 11 Character Batch Roadmap

## Purpose

Define how future passes should complete character coverage without doing one long Kaeya-style pipeline per character.

## Batch Principles

- Foundation batch size: 10-20 characters.
- Character story provenance batch size: 8-15 characters.
- Claim mapping batch size: 3-5 high-risk characters.
- Do not mix too many regions in one batch unless necessary.
- Do not update blocked characters without source packages.
- Separate Level 2 page normalization from Level 3 provenance and Level 4 claim mapping.

## Stop Conditions

The character layer is complete enough when:

- all playable/major characters are at Level 2;
- blocked characters are clearly marked and not upgraded by implication;
- source gaps are documented for every inventory character;
- no invalid source IDs are used;
- Level 4 claim mapping is done only for high-risk/core characters;
- no global wiki file becomes a project-specific story plan.
