## 3. Discovery Method & Skipped Entities

### Discovery Method
The inventory was compiled by scanning all registered book texts and quest summary files in `sources/book_texts/nod-krai/` and cross-referencing with the regional and faction governance documents created in Pass NK-03. Characters were checked for primary local source existence, existing page status, and global coverage plans.

### Characters Skipped
- **Nefer**: Skipped from active inventory. Topic/synthesis files link her to the "Voynich Guild," but since the guild lacks any local primary source support, Nefer is strictly skipped and blocked as `source_missing`.
- **Illuga, Jahoda, Linnea, Prune, Zibai**: Skipped. These entities exist in some global planning lists or unverified analysis summaries but have zero presence in the verified local books, quest transcripts, or governance records of the Nod-Krai region.

## 4. Scope & Reliability Limitations

1. **Quest Summary Constraints**: Quest summaries (`AQ-NK-0001` and `WQ-NK-0001`) are medium-reliability provenance records, not raw dialogue transcripts. They support only macro-level facts (e.g. character name, general faction affiliation, and presence in a subregion). They cannot support detailed personality claims, voice styles, relationship dynamics, or dialogue.
2. **Pre-History / Folklore Constraints**: Characters verified only through books (like `BK-0005` or `BK-0009`) are folkloric or historical. They must not be written as active, modern personas.
3. **No Direct Page Creation**: All character entity page files remain under the direct control of the global Full Character Foundation Plan.
