## Key Findings

### Already strong

Kaeya is the only character with a full Kaeya-style provenance and claim-mapping pipeline through Level 4. Existing Level 2 foundation pages are present for Aether, Albedo, Arlecchino, Columbina, Diluc, Dottore, Furina, Jean, Kaeya, Klee, Kẻ Lang Thang, Lisa, Lumine, Nahida, Neuvillette, Raiden Shogun, Sandrone, Tartaglia, Venti, and Zhongli. Dottore and Sandrone are pages, but they are blocked guard pages rather than source-rich foundations.

### Needs template normalization

Most existing pages predate the proposed Character Template v2 and should be normalized in future batches for consistent Audit Status, source_status, Voice Guide, OOC Risk Register, Safe/Unsafe Uses, and Sources sections. Kaeya should be used as the template/example, not as the only target.

### Needs source coverage check

Most inventory entries have local voice-line files but no character-story provenance. Future batches should check voice lines first, then add Level 2 pages, then run separate small provenance batches instead of one long Kaeya pipeline per character.

### Source-light / blocked

Dottore and Sandrone remain blocked_by_missing_source. Columbina remains audited_source_light / indirect_source_only, especially for Kuutar/Moon identity claims. Skirk is not an inventory character page target in this table but remains source_missing in the broader source governance notes. Nod-Krai later-region characters with missing voice files should stay guarded until sources exist.

### High-priority for Level 4 later

Archons, Traveler twins, Neuvillette, Wanderer/Kẻ Lang Thang, Albedo, Arlecchino, Tartaglia, Dottore, Columbina, Sandrone, Nicole, Varka, and any Abyss/Khaenri'ah/Descender-linked character require claim mapping only after source packages exist.
