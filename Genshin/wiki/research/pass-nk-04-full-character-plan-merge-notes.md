## 3. Required Preconditions for Upgrade

Before any character in the Nod-Krai batch can be upgraded from Level 0/1 to Level 2 (Character Foundation Page), the following preconditions must be fulfilled and verified:

1. **Raw Dialogue Transcript Ingestion**: Physical in-game dialog transcripts for Snezhnaya Archon Quest Act I (and any world quests) must be ingested and registered in the wiki. This is required to verify dialogue style, personal relationships, and voice profiles.
2. **Profile Source Ingestion**: Character stories and voice line data (`VL-*` and `CS-*` files) for the playable characters must be retrieved and logged in the workspace.
3. **Global Plan Activation**: The global Full Character Foundation Plan must formally schedule and activate the Nod-Krai batch. All page drafting must use the approved `_character-template-v2.md` system.
