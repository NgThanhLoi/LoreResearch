# Pass 11 Character Tier Map

## Tier Policy

Tier A - Core / High Lore Risk: Archons, Traveler twins, Abyss/Khaenri'ah-linked characters, Fatui/Harbingers, Irminsul/Descender/Dragon Sovereign-linked characters, and characters likely to break lore if written wrong.

Tier B - Major Playable / Region Anchors: playable characters with frequent fanfic utility, faction leaders, strong relationship networks, or region-anchor duties.

Tier C - Standard Playable: playable characters needing Level 2 foundation coverage but not deep claim mapping yet.

Tier D - Minor / Cameo / Source-light: minor, later-region, cameo, or limited-source characters that should stay at Level 0/1 until source checks justify more.

Tier X - Blocked / Do Not Upgrade: source_missing or source-light characters that must remain blocked or limited until source packages exist.
