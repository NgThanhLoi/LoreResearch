## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Fischl for Adventurers' Guild investigations and theatrical prophecy-like commentary.

### AU
Preserve her dramatic self-mythology or clearly explain its alteration.

### Romance
Respect the persona; intimacy may involve understanding rather than breaking it.

### Mystery / Detective
Oz and her observational flair work well for strange clues.

### Political / Faction
Use as guild field talent, not national leadership.

### Action / Battle
Use Electro/Oz coordination without treating Oz as omnipotent.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Persona/Oz translation can be funny without ridicule.

### Dark fantasy
Theatrical darkness can fit, but avoid making her truly sinister by default.

### OC-insert
An OC should learn her idiom rather than mock it.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
She responds in dramatic proclamations; Oz clarifies.
