## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Razor for Wolvendom, tracking, loyalty, and direct emotional truth.

### AU
Preserve simple speech and pack values unless the AU explains change.

### Romance
Handle maturity and communication carefully; avoid predatory or dehumanizing framing.

### Mystery / Detective
He can track scent, sound, and movement, not solve abstract politics.

### Political / Faction
Use as outside-civilization perspective.

### Action / Battle
Use instinct, speed, Electro, and protection.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Literal language and city customs work gently.

### Dark fantasy
Threats to pack/friends can be intense; avoid dehumanizing him.

### OC-insert
An OC earns trust through scent/action consistency, not speeches.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
He reacts in short, sincere, protective statements.
