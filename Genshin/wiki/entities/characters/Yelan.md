## Summary

Yelan is a Hydro-wielding intelligence operative based in Liyue who works in the shadows gathering information for the Qixing. She maintains multiple identities and operates the Yanshang Teahouse as a cover. Her voice lines reveal a personality defined by calculated detachment, preference for spicy stimuli, gambling habits, and a pragmatic approach to relationships built on mutual exchange of information. She values her independence, dislikes wasting energy on unnecessary work, and views most interactions through the lens of intelligence value.

## Safe Uses

- Writing Yelan gathering intelligence through social manipulation, cover identities, and information exchange while maintaining emotional distance
- Depicting her lazy/napping cycles contrasted with sudden high-efficiency bursts when a mission demands it
- Showing her relationships as mutually beneficial exchanges where genuine care exists beneath the transactional framing

## Unsafe Uses

- Portraying Yelan as emotionally dependent, easily manipulated, or needing rescue from danger she cannot handle alone
- Writing her as an omniscient spy master who already knows all secrets without effort or gaps in knowledge
- Presenting her Chasm backstory, ancestor sacrifice details, or full intelligence network scope as confirmed canon without story quest source verification
