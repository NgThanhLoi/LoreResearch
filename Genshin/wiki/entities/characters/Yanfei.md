## Summary

Yanfei is a half-adeptus legal advisor operating independently in Liyue Harbor. Her voice lines establish her as a highly confident, fast-talking professional who views law as both a tool for freedom and a binding force for social order. She was born after the Archon War ended, meaning she has no contract with the Geo Archon and lives freely among mortals. Her speech patterns reveal someone intellectually sharp, cheerful, and deeply committed to the principle that contracts and law maintain peace in Liyue.

## Safe Uses

- Writing Yanfei as a fast-talking, cheerful legal advisor who solves Liyue-specific contract disputes using creative interpretation of established law
- Depicting her as content with her half-adeptus nature, living freely without obligation to the Archon's contracts
- Showing her intellectual competitive streak with peers (Ningguang, Keqing) while maintaining warmth and professionalism

## Unsafe Uses

- Portraying her as an all-knowing legal authority whose expertise works seamlessly in every nation without study or adaptation
- Writing her as a powerful adeptus combatant on par with contracted adepti (Xiao, Ganyu in combat mode) without Story source confirmation
- Depicting her as deeply unhappy, ashamed of her heritage, or emotionally broken - contradicts her explicit voice line contentment
