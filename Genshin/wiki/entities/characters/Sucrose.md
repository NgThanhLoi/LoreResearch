Use Sucrose for lab analysis, specimens, shy technical help, and Albedo-adjacent work.

Preserve curiosity and politeness unless deliberately changed.

Use awkward trust and careful boundaries; avoid objectifying her shyness.

### Mystery / Detective
She can analyze samples, plants, residues, and experimental anomalies.

### Political / Faction
Use as a technical specialist, not a political leader.

Use Anemo and alchemical support within limits.
