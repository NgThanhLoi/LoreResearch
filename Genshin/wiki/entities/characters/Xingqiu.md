## Summary

Xingqiu is the second young master of the Feiyun Commerce Guild in Liyue, a wealthy merchant family. He presents himself publicly as a refined, bookish young scholar but privately pursues martial arts and chivalric ideals connected to the Guhua Clan. His voice lines reveal a personality that balances literary elegance with playful wit, a deep love of reading, and a genuine desire to help others. He maintains close relationships with several Liyue characters, particularly Chongyun, and guards his martial activities as a secret from casual acquaintances.

## Safe Uses

- Writing him as a well-read young man who balances scholarly refinement with hidden martial competence and genuine care for friends
