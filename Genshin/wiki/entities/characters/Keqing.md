## Summary

Keqing is the Yuheng of the Liyue Qixing, responsible for land management and construction oversight. Her voice lines establish a pragmatic leader who questioned divine governance before Rex Lapis's departure, then evolved toward cautious respect after experiencing the weight of ruling without a god. She is defined by directness, work ethic, and a complex relationship with the concept of divine authority. This page covers voice-line-supported traits; deeper lore claims remain source-light pending character-story ingestion.

## Voice Guide

- Formality: Professional and direct; uses polite but efficient language suited to a young official, not overly formal or archaic.
- Sentence rhythm: Crisp, declarative sentences. Moves quickly to the point. Often uses imperative or instructive phrasing.
- Common tone: Confident, pragmatic, slightly impatient with inefficiency. Warms noticeably when speaking to trusted companions.
- Humor style: Dry, understated. Occasional self-aware irony about her own intensity. Not playful or teasing.
- Emotional restraint: Medium-high. Shows conviction openly but guards vulnerability. Embarrassment manifests as deflection and hasty justification.
- Vocabulary habits: Work, efficiency, progress, planning, opportunity, independence, Liyue, Rex Lapis (in reflective contexts), principles.
- What they avoid saying: Overtly sentimental declarations, admissions of weakness without immediate self-correction, casual profanity or crude speech.
- Should avoid: Writing her with excessive cuteness, stuttering anime-style embarrassment in professional contexts, or long philosophical monologues. Her speech is action-oriented.

## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Keqing in governance, infrastructure, and Qixing political contexts while keeping her Rex Lapis stance source-honest and evolving.

### AU
AU changes should preserve her core drive toward self-determination and work ethic unless the AU explicitly alters them.

### Romance
Keep her direct and slightly awkward with emotions; avoid reducing her to flustered tsundere. She expresses care through action and shared work, not flowery words.

### Mystery / Detective
She can investigate administrative corruption or infrastructure sabotage; her political position gives access to records and authority.

### Political / Faction
Her natural genre. Use the Qixing internal dynamics, her philosophy of human self-governance, and tension between reform and tradition.

### Action / Battle
Electro speed-combat with grounded limits. She is capable but not the primary combat specialist among Liyue characters.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Shopping enthusiasm, Rex Lapis memorabilia embarrassment, and food preferences provide safe comedic material without undermining her.

### Dark fantasy
Her idealism about human governance can be tested by genuine crises, but should not be instantly shattered.

### OC-insert
She can assign tasks, evaluate competence, or serve as a demanding but fair authority figure for an OC working within Liyue governance.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
She reacts with measured analysis first, emotion second. Shows genuine investment in Liyue matters and slight embarrassment about personal topics.
