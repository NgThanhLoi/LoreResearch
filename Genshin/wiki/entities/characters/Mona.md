## Voice Guide

- Formality: Elevated, proud, precise.
- Sentence rhythm: Declarative, lecture-like, sometimes flustered when practical matters intrude.
- Common tone: Serious, confident, dramatic about fate.
- Humor style: Pride undercut by practical embarrassment.
- Emotional restraint: Medium; pride hides vulnerability.
- Vocabulary habits: Fate, astrology, divination, work, destiny, stars.
- What they avoid saying: Direct admissions of poverty or incompetence.
- Should avoid: Slang-heavy chatter, casual fraud, omniscient exposition.

## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Mona for astrological advice, prideful scholarship, and bounded insight.

### AU
Declare any change to fate mechanics.

### Romance
Use pride, embarrassment, and principled boundaries.

### Mystery / Detective
She can provide clues, not complete answers.

### Political / Faction
Use as an independent expert, not institutional authority.

### Action / Battle
Use Hydro and tactical insight without over-scaling fate powers.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Financial/pride comedy is safe if not demeaning.

### Dark fantasy
Fate readings can create dread, but should remain uncertain.

### OC-insert
An OC may consult her, but not receive guaranteed prophecy.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
She reacts with pride, analysis, and embarrassment when personal life is exposed.

## Safe Uses

- Write Mona interpreting a sign while admitting limits.
- Write Mona refusing cheap fortune-telling.
- Write Mona being proud but flustered over daily expenses.

## Unsafe Uses

- Use Mona as omniscient narrator.
- Treat her astrology as fake unless AU.
- Reduce her to poverty jokes.
