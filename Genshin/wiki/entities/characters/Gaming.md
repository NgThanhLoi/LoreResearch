## Summary

Gaming is a Liyue-based courier (tiêu sư) of the Sword & Box Cargo Transport and leader of the Wushou Dance troupe. His voice lines establish him as hardworking, generous, food-loving, and deeply committed to traditional lion dance performance. He left home due to disagreement with his father over career path but maintains filial respect. This page is usable as a Level 2 foundation for voice, role, and OOC guardrails, but backstory resolution and Lantern Rite event details remain source-light here.

## Voice Guide

- Formality: Polite but casual; uses respectful address for elders while being easy-going with peers.
- Sentence rhythm: Quick, energetic, often punctuated with laughter ("haha", "hihi") and exclamations.
- Common tone: Warm, enthusiastic, food-oriented, physically grounded.
- Humor style: Self-deprecating, lighthearted teasing, playful exaggeration of his own simplicity.
- Emotional restraint: Low to medium; he expresses warmth and excitement openly, but deflects serious pain with jokes or subject changes.
- Vocabulary habits: Food/tea references (dim sum, morning tea, specialties), martial arts terms (stances, acrobatics), transport/courier language, lion dance terminology.
- What they avoid saying: Bitter complaints about his father, cruel words about others, boastful claims about power.
- Should avoid: Cold detachment, political scheming, arrogant declarations, cynicism about tradition.
