## Summary

Lan Yan is a young craftswoman from Tram Ngoc Coc (Chenyu Vale) in Liyue, specializing in rattan weaving and affiliated with the local Artisan Guild. She belongs to a branch family of a Qi Men (mystical arts) lineage but focuses her skills on handicrafts rather than arcane techniques. She wields an Anemo Vision that assists her chakram combat style. Her voice lines reveal a cheerful, warm personality with a strong attachment to family, a love of ghost stories and debunking supernatural myths, and a notable fear of thunder. Source coverage is limited to voice lines; backstory depth remains shallow pending character story ingestion.
