## Summary

Chongyun is a young exorcist from a long-established Liyue family lineage of spirit-warding practitioners. His voice lines establish him as earnest, disciplined, and dedicated to his craft, with a particular frustration that his innate constitution (Thuan Duong Chi The / Congenital Positivity) drives away evil spirits before he can confront them. He maintains a cold-oriented lifestyle to suppress his yang energy and avoids hot or spicy foods. His speech is polite, sincere, and grounded in exorcism tradition, with moments of awkward embarrassment when his condition is discussed.

## Power / Lore Constraints

- Known abilities: Cryo Vision, martial arts (greatsword), talisman/charm-based exorcism techniques, congenital yang constitution that passively repels evil spirits.
- Limits: Has never actually confronted a spirit directly due to his yang aura. Vision is a tool, not a crutch; he emphasizes traditional martial skill and charms over Vision reliance.
- Misuse risks: Do not treat his yang constitution as an active combat power or unlimited spirit-detection radar. Do not scale him above standard playable-character level.
- Power-scaling warnings: He is a young practitioner still training; do not write him as a master-level exorcist or equal to adepti.
- Source status: mixed
- Notes: Detailed exorcism mechanics and yang energy limits require character-story provenance.

## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Chongyun investigating supernatural reports, training his martial arts, or navigating Liyue commissions while managing his condition.

### AU
Preserve his earnestness, discipline, and the core tension of wanting to prove himself through skill rather than passive ability.

### Romance
Keep him sincere and somewhat awkward; avoid reducing him to a blushing caricature or making his condition purely comic.

### Mystery / Detective
He works well investigating supernatural cases, following leads on evil spirits, and debunking false hauntings.

### Political / Faction
He is an independent practitioner, not a political actor; use him as a perspective on traditional Liyue spiritual practice.

### Action / Battle
Use martial arts, Cryo abilities, and talisman techniques with grounded limits; his yang aura is passive, not a battle move.

### Comedy / Slice of life
His cold-food lifestyle, accidental spirit-repelling, and earnest personality create natural situational comedy.

### Dark fantasy
Let his dedication be tested by genuine supernatural threat without instantly solving everything via passive yang aura.

### OC-insert
He can offer exorcism services, companionship on spirit hunts, or introduction to Liyue's supernatural side.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
He reacts with composed sincerity; shows frustration at false leads and genuine excitement at real supernatural findings.
