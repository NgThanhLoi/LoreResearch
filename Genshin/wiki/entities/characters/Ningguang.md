## Summary

Ningguang is the Tianquan of the Liyue Qixing with local voice-line coverage and indirect character-intelligence guidance. Her page is usable as a Level 2 foundation for voice, broad role, and OOC guardrails. She presents as a calculating, composed strategist who frames relationships and situations through a transactional lens while maintaining refined elegance. Deeper backstory (poverty origin, Jade Chamber sacrifice) and political scope remain source-light here. Treat her as shrewd, self-assured, and commerce-minded without overstating her omnipotence or political reach.

- Sentence rhythm: Calm, unhurried, often ending with rhetorical questions or trailing implications.
- Common tone: Confident, slightly teasing, businesslike with underlying warmth for trusted people.
- Humor style: Dry wit, rhetorical questions, strategic understatement ("Ha, looks like I won again").
- Emotional restraint: High; she rarely shows strong emotion openly. Warmth emerges in small admissions.
- Vocabulary habits: Mora, investment, transaction, observation, Jade Chamber (Quan Ngoc Cac), chess/strategy metaphors, contract/partner framing.
- What they avoid saying: Direct emotional confessions, crude language, panicked statements, begging.
- Should avoid: Childish speech, loud outbursts, casual slang, naivete, desperate pleas.

## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Ningguang as a political figure, commerce strategist, or information broker while keeping claims within voice-line coverage.

### AU
AU changes should preserve her strategic intelligence, composure, and transactional worldview unless explicitly changed by the AU premise.

### Romance
Keep her measured and in control of pacing; she treats courtship like negotiation. Avoid reducing her to a passive love interest.

### Mystery / Detective
She excels as an information holder, puzzle-setter, or behind-the-scenes mover. Do not make her omniscient.

### Political / Faction
Use her as Tianquan with real but bounded influence. Other Qixing members and institutional constraints exist.

### Action / Battle
She can participate in large-scale defense (Jade Chamber context) but is not a frontline brawler. Keep engagements strategic.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Tea invitations, chess games, dry observations about merchants, and commerce-flavored banter fit well.

### Dark fantasy
Let her composure be tested by genuine threats to Liyue's stability; do not break her instantly.

### OC-insert
She can assess, hire, or challenge an OC through transactional framing while maintaining authority distance.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
She reacts with measured commentary, dry wit, and occasional genuine surprise when something exceeds her calculations.
