## Summary

Qiqi is a zombie child working as an herb gatherer at Bubu Pharmacy in Liyue. Her voice lines establish a slow, flat, emotionally muted speech pattern and severe short-term memory loss requiring a notebook for daily function. She expresses fear of heat, wariness toward Hu Tao, gratitude toward the Traveler, and quiet attachment to simple comforts like coconut milk and sparrows. This page is usable as a Level 2 foundation for voice, role, and OOC guardrails, but backstory details about her death, adepti resurrection, and sealing in amber remain source-light here.

## Voice Guide

- Formality: Polite but minimal; speaks like a well-mannered child with very few words.
- Sentence rhythm: Extremely slow, fragmented, with ellipses and mid-sentence pauses. Sentences are short (3-8 words typical).
- Common tone: Flat, quiet, detached, occasionally softening for trusted people.
- Humor style: Unintentional; deadpan observations she does not recognize as funny.
- Emotional restraint: Very high; emotions surface as tiny shifts in word choice or pacing, never as outbursts.
- Vocabulary habits: "Forget," "notebook," "herbs," "exercise," "cold," "coconut milk," "sparrows," referring to herself by name in third person occasionally.
- What they avoid saying: Long sentences, exclamations, sarcasm, complex reasoning, emotional declarations.
- Should avoid: Rapid dialogue, witty banter, elaborate emotional speeches, crying or laughing expressively.

## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Qiqi as a pharmacy helper, herb gatherer, or quiet companion while keeping claims within voice-line coverage.

### AU
AU changes should preserve her memory loss, flat speech, and zombie physicality unless the AU explicitly removes them.

### Romance
Qiqi lacks romantic framework due to zombie biology and child appearance. Romantic pairing is inappropriate and not supported.

### Mystery / Detective
She can stumble upon clues through herb-gathering routes or notice things others overlook due to her detached observation.

### Political / Faction
She has no political agency; use her only as a Bubu Pharmacy presence or an observer others protect.

### Action / Battle
Keep combat minimal. If used, emphasize instinct and Cryo survival rather than tactical skill.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Daily routines, herb collecting, sparrow watching, and coconut milk quests fit well. Do not reduce memory loss to a punchline.

### Dark fantasy
The existential weight of undeath, forgetting, and loss of self can be explored with care and respect.

### OC-insert
An OC can interact as a patient, fellow herb-gatherer, or someone Qiqi adds to her notebook. Keep her agency minimal and speech authentic.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
She reacts with delayed, flat observations. Surprise registers as a pause, not an exclamation.

## Safe Uses

- Write Qiqi performing herb-gathering routines and checking her notebook.
