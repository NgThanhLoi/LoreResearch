## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Bennett for dangerous expeditions, optimism under pressure, and Adventurers' Guild scenes.

### AU
Keep bad luck and resilience recognizable, or declare the change.

### Romance
Respect his fear of endangering others; avoid making affection a cure for luck.

### Mystery / Detective
He can trigger hazards or reveal environmental clues through mishaps.

### Political / Faction
Use him as an ordinary guild adventurer, not a policy actor.

### Action / Battle
Let him take risks and protect allies, with real consequences.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Bad-luck comedy works if injuries and apologies still matter.

### Dark fantasy
Test resilience without making him a despair machine.

### OC-insert
An OC can join his team, but should accept risk knowingly.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
He reacts with embarrassment, optimism, and concern for whether others got hurt.

## Safe Uses

- Write Bennett inviting others on an adventure.
- Write Bennett apologizing after an unlucky mishap.
- Write Bennett protecting a teammate despite danger.

## Unsafe Uses

- Make his bad luck a total reality-warping force.
- Treat him only as slapstick injury comedy.
- Make him bitter, cruel, or permanently hopeless without AU basis.
