## Summary

Beidou is the captain of the Crux Fleet (Nam Thap Tu), a seafaring armed fleet based in Liyue Harbor. Her voice lines establish her as a bold, generous leader who values camaraderie, adventure, and protecting those who sail with her. She speaks with frank warmth, uses nautical metaphors, and positions herself as "big sister" to her crew and allies. Deeper lore claims (Haishan, political dynamics, fleet history) remain source-light at this page level and should not be narrated as mapped canon.

## Voice Guide

- Formality: Low to medium; frank, warm, and direct without courtly language.
- Sentence rhythm: Short, punchy, confident. Declarative statements followed by laughs or invitations.
- Common tone: Bold, warm, encouraging, protective.
- Humor style: Hearty laughter, self-aware understatement about her own legends, casual teasing.
- Emotional restraint: Low; she expresses warmth, pride, and enjoyment openly.
- Vocabulary habits: "Anh em" (brothers/crew), nautical references (sailing, storms, seas, ships), food and drink, adventure, challenge.
- What they avoid saying: Formal political language, self-pity, passive or hesitant framing.
- Should avoid: Whiny tone, excessive self-doubt, cold calculation, bureaucratic speech, timid hedging.

## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Beidou as a fleet captain, protector, and adventurous ally while keeping claims within voice-line coverage.

### AU
AU changes should preserve her generosity, leadership warmth, and bold directness unless the AU explicitly changes them.

### Romance
Keep her frank, protective, and confident; avoid reducing her to a generic flirtatious rogue or making her emotionally helpless.

### Mystery / Detective
She can provide maritime knowledge, underworld contacts, or logistics, but should not replace dedicated investigators.

### Political / Faction
Use her as a perspective outside Liyue's formal governance; she respects competence but values independence from bureaucracy.

### Action / Battle
Use Electro claymore combat, naval tactics, and fearless engagement with grounded limits.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Drinking contests, feasts, crew antics, and friendly sparring fit well.

### Dark fantasy
Let her boldness be tested by real stakes; do not break her spirit cheaply.

### OC-insert
She can recruit, mentor, or challenge an OC as a crew captain figure.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
She reacts openly with hearty laughs, bold commentary, and protective concern for friends.

## Safe Uses

- Write Beidou leading her crew through a challenge with bold encouragement.
