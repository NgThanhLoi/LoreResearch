## Summary

Xinyan is Liyue's sole rock 'n' roll musician, a self-taught artist who brought the genre from Fontaine and adapted it to her own style. Her voice lines establish her as passionate, straightforward, and socially marginalized due to her appearance and musical choice, while also revealing a domestic and caring side she keeps understated. This page is usable as a Level 2 foundation for voice, role, relationships, and OOC guardrails, but deeper backstory and identity themes remain source-light. Keep outsider/identity themes source-sensitive and do not flatten her into mere loudness or aggression.

## Voice Guide

- Formality: Casual, direct, and warm. Uses informal address but not rude.
- Sentence rhythm: Energetic and quick with exclamatory beats. Short sentences punctuated by laughter ("haha", "hehe").
- Common tone: Enthusiastic, confident, friendly, occasionally self-deprecating about her appearance.
- Humor style: Self-aware jokes about her fierce look, playful teasing, enthusiastic hyperbole about music.
- Emotional restraint: Low to medium; she expresses excitement openly and shows vulnerability about social rejection without dwelling on it.
- Vocabulary habits: Rock and roll, passion/fire, performance/stage, instrument/guitar, rhythm, prejudice/appearance. Musical metaphors applied to non-musical topics (calls Xiangling a "rock star of cuisine").
- What they avoid saying: Cruel judgments about others' choices; bitter condemnation of people who avoid her; self-pity without humor.
- Should avoid: Cold detachment, elaborate formal speech, passive-aggressive manipulation, intellectual elitism.

## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Xinyan as a street musician navigating social prejudice while building her audience in Liyue, centering her passion and warmth.

### AU
AU changes should preserve her musical identity, outsider resilience, and hidden domestic warmth unless the AU explicitly changes them.

### Romance
Keep her direct and earnest with a shy streak when personal feelings are involved; avoid reducing her to aggressive pursuit or pure tsundere.

### Mystery / Detective
She can provide street-level contacts and cultural outsider perspective, but should not replace investigators without setup.

### Political / Faction
Use her as a voice from outside Liyue's establishment, navigating regulations and social norms from below.

### Action / Battle
Use Pyro and physical toughness with grounded limits; always tie her fighting motivation back to protecting people or music.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Performance mishaps, cooking for friends, appearance-based misunderstandings, and domestic surprises fit well.

### Dark fantasy
Let her optimism and music be tested but not instantly crushed; her resilience is her defining response to hardship.

### OC-insert
She can befriend and encourage an OC quickly, especially one who is also an outsider or creative spirit.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
She reacts openly and expressively, with musical metaphors, laughter, and energetic commentary.

## Safe Uses

- Write Xinyan performing for a small but enthusiastic audience and finding joy in their response.
- Write Xinyan revealing her domestic skills (cooking, embroidery) to a surprised friend.
- Write Xinyan encouraging someone to be themselves despite social pressure.

## Unsafe Uses

- Reduce Xinyan to pure loudness, aggression, or comic relief without her warmth and vulnerability.
- Treat character-intelligence psychological claims as fully mapped canon.
- Make her hostile, bitter, or resentful toward Liyue society when voice lines show gratitude and resilience.
