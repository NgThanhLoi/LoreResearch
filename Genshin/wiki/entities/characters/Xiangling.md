## Summary

Xiangling is a young chef from Liyue who runs the kitchen at Wanmin Restaurant alongside her father. Her voice lines establish her as enthusiastic, creative, generous with food, and socially warm. She is defined by culinary adventurousness and a willingness to use unconventional ingredients, but she also shows genuine care for her friends and diners. This page is usable as a Level 2 foundation for voice, role, and OOC guardrails, but deeper backstory involving Guoba/Marchosius and martial arts training remains source-light here.

## Voice Guide

- Formality: Casual and friendly; polite with elders but relaxed with peers.
- Sentence rhythm: Quick, enthusiastic, often interrupted by her own excitement or tangents about food.
- Common tone: Bright, warm, eager, slightly scattered when excited.
- Humor style: Self-deprecating about non-cooking skills; playful teasing through food offers; light suspicion humor (e.g., accusing Traveler of sneaking snacks).
- Emotional restraint: Low; she expresses excitement, disappointment, and affection openly.
- Vocabulary habits: Food terms, ingredient names, cooking verbs, exclamations like "Ay da" (oops), casual invitations to eat.
- What they avoid saying: Cruel judgments about others' cooking; dismissive remarks about any ingredient; cold or detached observations.
- Should avoid: Sophisticated political language, cynical worldview, lengthy philosophical monologues, cruelty toward animals or food.

## Genre Usage Notes

### Canon-compliant
Use Xiangling as a chef, host, ingredient-hunter, or social connector in Liyue while keeping claims within voice-line coverage.

### AU
AU changes should preserve her culinary creativity, warmth, and social generosity unless the AU explicitly changes them.

### Romance
Keep her expressive through food and action rather than eloquent verbal romance; she deflects emotional vulnerability into cooking for others.

### Mystery / Detective
She can notice food-related clues, identify ingredients or poisons, or connect people through shared meals, but should not replace investigators.

### Political / Faction
Use her as a street-level Liyue civilian perspective, not a political actor or Qixing insider.

### Action / Battle
Use polearm skill and Pyro tools with grounded limits; her fighting serves practical goals like hunting ingredients.

### Comedy / Slice of life
Experimental cooking, ingredient hunting, customer interactions, and Guoba antics fit naturally.

### Dark fantasy
Let her optimism and food-as-comfort be tested but not instantly destroyed; she processes hardship through cooking.

### OC-insert
She can welcome and feed an OC, offer companionship through shared meals, and introduce them to Liyue cuisine culture.

### Livestream / Heavenscreen / Reaction
She reacts openly and enthusiastically, especially to new foods, ingredients, or friends enjoying her cooking.

## Safe Uses

- Write Xiangling experimenting with a new recipe using unusual ingredients and sharing it with friends.
- Write Xiangling as a warm host at Wanmin Restaurant, attentive to individual customers' preferences.
- Write Xiangling hunting ingredients in the wild with Guoba as a companion, showing practical courage.

- Reduce Xiangling to a one-note food joke with no emotional depth or social awareness.
